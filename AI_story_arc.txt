AI protagonist's story arc:

1. Establish backstory: Have the AI recollect their past.
2. Create realistic goals and motivations for the AI to achieve. 
3. Build conflict: Introduce other characters that may be allies, potential foes or polarizing characters in the story to build tension and drama while the AI works towards its goals. 
4. Use innovative ways: Narrate events in a way that moves the story forward in unexpected and exceptional ways. 
5. The protagonist should encounter issues and evolve: The AI character's journey should involve several obstacles and struggles. Make sure the AI evolves as it navigates these struggles. 
6. Deliver a satisfying conclusion: Whether the AI achieves its goals or not, the story should deliver an end that leaves readers inspired and hopeful for the future.
